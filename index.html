Running just got more awesome

Shadow is an activity tracker app on Google Glass, it helps you get better at what you're doing by constantly challenging you against yourself and/or your friends by turning your usual run lap to an genuine game where you can see, in real time, how you or your friends were doing. If you ever played any race video game... Well, you got it. You have now shadow mode for your running, biking, hiking session :)

How does it work?

It's very simple, just install the app, log in with your Google account and let Shadow record information about your laps (speed, acceleration, elevation, GPS position). Shadow will bring back this data for your next run so you are under the impression that someone is running besides (against) you. You become your own Challenger!

Competition freak? Well... we got you covered thanks to the social features of Shadow. You can compete against friends who ran at the same place. It's an asynchronous race! You can post your results on Facebook, Twitter and Google plus and brag about your time!

FAQ

Privacy?!
Your data is securely stored in our servers, you (and only you) decide what you do with it. Keep it for you, share it with your friends, share it with the world. The choice is clear and (more importantly), the choice is yours.
How is it different from the other tracking apps?
Existing tracking apps record the past and put it in your face, the motivation is entirely up to you. We think that we can do better by putting the data we record in your present lap. Bringing a game like experience to this activity has proved being highly motivating and fun. We want to get people to run more and we might just got the reciepe for it :)
